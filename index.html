Zoidac Sign

Aries (March 21 - April 19)

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, symbolizing new beginnings and a pioneering spirit. They're driven, confident, and unafraid to take on new challenges. Aries individuals are natural leaders, often inspiring others with their enthusiasm and courage.

Taurus (April 20 - May 20)

Taurus is known for their practicality, reliability, and strong sense of loyalty. They value stability, comfort, and the finer things in life. While they can be stubborn, their determination often leads them to achieve their goals.

Gemini (May 21 - June 20)

Gemini is characterized by their adaptability, curiosity, and excellent communication skills. They have a dual nature, which makes them versatile and sociable. Geminis are always eager to learn new things and enjoy engaging in lively conversations.

Cancer (June 21 - July 22)

Cancer is deeply connected to their emotions and home life. They are nurturing, protective, and highly intuitive. Cancers are empathetic and often put the needs of others before their own, making them excellent caregivers and friends

Leo (July 23 - August 22)

Leo is the sign of the lion, symbolizing strength, courage, and leadership. Leos are confident, charismatic, and love being in the spotlight. They have a generous nature and a strong desire to make a lasting impact.

Virgo (August 23 - September 22)

Virgo is known for their meticulous attention to detail and analytical mindset. They are practical, organized, and always strive for perfection. Virgos have a deep sense of duty and are often the ones who keep things running smoothly behind the scenes.

Libra (September 23 - October 22)

Libra is the sign of balance and harmony. They are diplomatic, fair-minded, and have a strong sense of justice. Libras value relationships and are always seeking to create peace and equilibrium in their interactions.

Scorpio (October 23 - November 21)

Scorpio is intense, passionate, and mysterious. They are known for their emotional depth and resilience. Scorpios are fiercely loyal and protective of those they care about, and they possess a strong desire for transformation and growth.

Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21)

Sagittarius is the adventurer of the zodiac. They are optimistic, independent, and have a deep love for exploration and discovery. Sagittarians are philosophical and enjoy seeking out new experiences and knowledge.

Capricorn (December 22 - January 19)

Capricorn is ambitious, disciplined, and highly focused on achieving their goals. They value tradition, hard work, and perseverance. Capricorns are natural leaders who excel in structured environments and are often seen as pillars of strength.

Aquarius (January 20 - February 18)

Aquarius is innovative, independent, and future-oriented. They are known for their progressive ideas and humanitarian outlook. Aquarians are often ahead of their time, embracing change and advocating for social reform.

Pisces (February 19 - March 20)

Pisces is the most intuitive and compassionate sign of the zodiac. They are deeply empathetic, creative, and often have a strong connection to the spiritual realm. Pisceans are dreamers who are in tune with their emotions and the emotions of others.
